🍪 Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

📋 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you interact with pages, and provide a better user experience. Cookies themselves cannot run programs or deliver viruses — they are just data files.

PixelCraft AI uses cookies strictly to improve your experience and to ensure our services function correctly. We never use cookies to collect sensitive personal information.

🔐 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to operate. Without them, core features like navigation, security, and session management would not work. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
pc_consent_v1 Stores your cookie consent preferences so you are not asked repeatedly. 1 year Essential
pc_consent_prefs_v1 Stores your granular cookie preferences (analytics, ads, social). 1 year Essential
connect.sid Session cookie for authentication when signed in with Google. Session Essential

📊 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use PixelCraft AI — which tools are popular, how long people stay, and where they come from. All data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out at any time.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Registers a unique ID to generate statistical data on how you use the website. 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Used to persist session state between page requests. 2 years
_gid Google Analytics Registers a unique ID that is used to generate statistical data. 24 hours

Provider privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy ↗

📢 Advertising Cookies (Optional)

When you have consented to ads, Google AdSense may set cookies to display personalized advertisements. These help keep PixelCraft AI free to use.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
__gads, __gpi Google AdSense Registers user data for targeted advertising purposes. 1 year

🌐 Third-Party Services

PixelCraft AI integrates with the following third-party services which may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts — For loading web fonts. No personal data is collected.
  • Google Analytics — For anonymous usage analytics (opt-in).
  • Google AdSense — For serving advertisements (opt-in).

  • Render.com — Our hosting provider may set infrastructure-level cookies.

⚙️ How to Manage Cookies

You are in full control of your cookie preferences. Here are your options:

  • Our consent tool — Click the "Cookie Preferences" badge in the corner of any page to update your choices instantly.
  • Browser settings — You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may break some features.
  • Opt-out tools — Use the NAI opt-out tool ↗ or DAA opt-out tool ↗ to control advertising cookies.
